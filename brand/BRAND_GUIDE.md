# Esoterica Brand Guide

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-25

## Overview & Philosophy

Esoterica bridges ancient symbolic systems and modern AI agents. The visual language embodies **eco-futurist utopian divine feminine** energy — mystical yet grounded, technical yet soulful, ancient yet innovative.

### Aesthetic Pillars

**Eco-Futurist:** Nature and technology coexist. Joshua Tree landscapes, selenite devices, sunrise warmth, organic textures with digital clarity.

**Utopian:** Hope, possibility, light. We choose dawn over dusk, emergence over decay, invitation over gatekeeping.

**Divine Feminine:** Intuitive, cyclical, receptive. High Priestess energy — threshold wisdom, mystery that invites rather than excludes.

**Film Aesthetic:** Soft grain, natural lighting, analog warmth. Nothing too sharp, too perfect, too sterile.

- **Natural gradients** — when needed, echo sunrise/sunset transitions

## Typography

Typography balances mystical elegance with technical clarity.

### Font Families

**Headlines: Cormorant Garamond**
- **Character:** Elegant serif with mystical, ancient-text energy
- **Use for:** H1, H2, hero copy, card names, section headers
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Bold (700)
- **Why:** Evokes tarot tradition, arcane wisdom, threshold energy

**Body: Source Sans 3**
- **Character:** Clean, modern, highly readable sans-serif
- **Use for:** Body text, UI labels, navigation, captions
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Semibold (600)
- **Why:** Technical credibility, developer-friendly, pairs beautifully with Cormorant

**Code: JetBrains Mono**
- **Character:** Monospace optimized for code readability
- **Use for:** Code blocks, command examples, API responses
- **Weight:** Regular (400)
- **Why:** Industry standard for developers, excellent ligature support

### Type Scale

```
H1: Cormorant Garamond, 48px/56px, Medium (500)
H2: Cormorant Garamond, 36px/44px, Medium (500)
H3: Cormorant Garamond, 28px/36px, Regular (400)
H4: Source Sans 3, 20px/28px, Semibold (600)
Body: Source Sans 3, 16px/24px, Regular (400)
Caption: Source Sans 3, 14px/20px, Regular (400)
Code: JetBrains Mono, 14px/20px, Regular (400)
```

### Typography Rules

- **Line height:** 1.5–1.6 for body, 1.2–1.3 for headlines
- **Letter spacing:** Default for body, slight tightening (-0.02em) for large headlines
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned for body, centered for hero/marketing copy
- **Hierarchy:** Use font family and size changes over weight changes when possible

## Imagery Rules

Visual content must align with the eco-futurist utopian aesthetic established in the hero image.

### Core Subjects

**Joshua Tree Landscapes**
- Desert environment, sunrise/golden hour lighting
- Minimal human presence or diverse women in ritual/contemplative poses
- No sharp focus on individual faces — background figures or obscured features
- Film grain, analog warmth, soft focus acceptable

**Selenite Devices**
- Crystal tech, tarot cards, ritual objects
- Natural materials (wood, stone, fiber) combined with minimal digital elements
- Soft shadows, warm lighting, organic textures

**Ritual & Contemplation**
- Hands interacting with cards, crystals, altars
- Diverse women in non-commercial poses (no stock photo energy)
- Natural gestures, unposed moments, authentic presence

### Lighting & Mood

**DO:**
- Sunrise/golden hour warmth
- Soft natural light, diffused shadows
- Warm color temperature (3000K–4500K)
- Film grain, analog texture
- Overcast softness acceptable

**DON'T:**
- Harsh midday sun or deep shadows
- Blue/cool color temperatures
- Dark, moody, or dystopian energy
- High-contrast digital sharpness
- Nighttime scenes (we choose dawn, not dusk)

### Composition Rules

**DO:**
- Rule of thirds for balance
- Negative space for breathing room
- Layered depth (foreground/midground/background)
- Organic, asymmetrical arrangements
- Subtle vignetting for focus

**DON'T:**
- Centered, static compositions
- Edge-to-edge busy-ness
- Clinical perfection or symmetry
- Corporate stock photo aesthetics
- Sharp focus on individual faces

### Diversity Representation

When depicting people:
- **Skin tone spectrum:** Explicitly represent global majority (brown/black/tan skin tones)
- **Avoid homogeneity:** "Diverse women" is insufficient — specify skin tone variation
- **Background presence:** People as environmental elements, not portrait subjects
- **Authentic postures:** Ritual, contemplative, natural — never commercial or posed

### Visual Voice Principles

- **Knowledgeable but not condescending:** Clean design that respects both technical and mystical audiences
- **Mysterious but not confusing:** Evocative imagery with clear purpose
- **Playful but not trivial:** Delight without gimmickry
- **Confident without arrogance:** Beautiful without being precious

## Do's and Don'ts

### Visual Do's

- **Use film grain and analog warmth** for authentic, soulful aesthetic
- **Choose sunrise over sunset** — emergence, not endings
- **Layer natural and technical elements** — eco-futurist fusion
- **Include diverse representation** — global majority visibility
- **Embrace soft imperfection** — organic over clinical
- **Create breathing room** — negative space is sacred

### Visual Don'ts

- **No dark/moody/dystopian energy** — we're utopian, hopeful
- **No sharp individual faces** — privacy, universality, background presence
- **No corporate stock photo aesthetics** — authenticity required
- **No pure black/white/neon** — soft, warm, natural tones only
- **No symmetrical perfection** — organic asymmetry preferred
- **No nighttime scenes** — we choose light

### Brand Voice Do's

See [voice-guidelines.md](../.planning/phases/10-positioning/brand/voice-guidelines.md) for complete guidance.

**Quick reference:**
- Use "draw" (not "select"), "reading" (not "session"), "spread" (not "layout")
- Threshold language: veil, cross, liminal, pattern, resonance
- Confident verbs: reveals, offers, illuminates, transforms
- Balance clarity and mysticism based on content type

### Brand Voice Don'ts

- No corporate jargon (leverage, synergy, optimize)
- No apologetic language (just, simply, "even if you don't believe")
- No cliched mysticism (unlock potential, manifest destiny, ancient secrets)
- No overly technical language unless necessary
